Biography

I am an Assistant Professor in the School of Computers at Guangdong University of Technology. I received my B.S. degree in Statistic and Ph.D. degree in Computer Science from Guangdong University of Technology, where I was very fortunate to be advised by Prof. Ruichu Cai. My research interests cover various topics, including causality, causality-inspired machine learning. I have served as PC member of ICML(2022-2025), NeurIPS (2022-2025), ICLR (2024-2025), AAAI (2021-2025), IJCAI (2023-2025), UAI (2022-2025), AISTATS (2025), etc.
